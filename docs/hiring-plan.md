## Recruiting Strategy

### Recruiting Channels

**For VPs & Senior Hires**:
- Executive search firm ($50-75k per placement)
- Warm network referrals (50% of hires, best quality)
- LinkedIn recruiter lite ($5-10k/month)

**For Engineers & Mid-Level**:
- Employee referral program ($5k per hire)
- LinkedIn recruiting (5-10 inbound per week expected)
- University recruiting (for junior positions)
- Bootcamp partnerships

**For Sales / CS**:
- Industry-specific recruiters
- LinkedIn recruiting
- Internal transfers (ops → architects)

### Recruiting Timeline

**Month 1-2**: Establish recruiting infrastructure
- Hire recruiting lead (0.5 FTE, part-time)
- Create job descriptions (20+ roles)
- Launch employee referral program ($5k per successful hire)
- Partner with 2-3 executive search firms (for VPs)

**Month 3-12**: Continuous recruiting
- Recruit 5-8 per month (120 inbound, 20 interviews, 5-8 offers, 4-5 accepts)
- Build recruiting pipeline (always have 10+ in-process)
- Track metrics: time-to-hire, offer accept rate, 6-month retention

## Onboarding & Culture

### Onboarding Program (2 weeks)

**Week 1** (5 days):
- Day 1: Office setup, systems (laptop, email, GitHub)
- Day 2: Product overview (watch Disney case study, read specs)
- Day 3: Engineering deep-dive (architecture, deployment, testing)
- Day 4: Customer deep-dive (sales calls, customer interviews, support tickets)
- Day 5: Team socials + 1:1s with all VPs

**Week 2** (5 days):
- Assigned buddy (mentor from team)
- Start on real project (small, achievable, with buddy support)
- Daily 1:1 check-ins (engineering manager or VP)
- Lunch-and-learns (4 sessions: folk strategy, specifications, compliance, roadmap)

### Culture & Values

**Core Values**:
1. **Determinism** - Prove it; don't hope for it
2. **Transparency** - Open books, open communication, no silos
3. **Ownership** - Take on hard problems, own outcomes
4. **Customer Obsession** - Customers are co-designers, not users
5. **Continuous Learning** - Folk strategy, mathematics, engineering

**Cultural Rituals**:
- Weekly all-hands (transparent metrics + wins + learning)
- Monthly team outing (hybrid remote/in-person)
- Quarterly offsite (2 days, strategy + team building)
- Annual company retreat (3 days, culture + strategy refresh)

### Retention & Growth

**6-Month Reviews**:
- Performance assessment (100% expectations met? >100%?)
- Compensation adjustment (raise if exceeding)
- Growth plan (next role, skills to develop)

**Annual Performance Review**:
- 360-degree feedback (peer + manager + direct reports)
- Compensation refresh (market adjustment + merit)
- Career development (promotion track, internal transfers)

**Target Retention**: 90%+ (low churn, especially for top performers)

## Leadership Hiring Criteria

### VP Engineering
**Must-Have**:
- 8+ years Rust or low-level systems programming
- Experience scaling technical teams (10-50 engineers)
- RDF / knowledge graph exposure (or hunger to learn)

**Nice-to-Have**:
- Infrastructure automation background (Terraform, Ansible)
- Distributed systems expertise
- Open-source contribution history

**Interview Process**:
- Phone screen (30 min, recruiter)
- Technical deep-dive (90 min, CEO + 2 senior engineers)
- System design (90 min, whiteboard architecture question)
- Culture fit (60 min, existing team members)
- Reference checks (3 references, probing on leadership)

### VP Sales
**Must-Have**:
- 10+ years B2B SaaS sales (5+ years quota-carrying)
- $10M+ ARR selling enterprise infrastructure
- Track record: 150%+ quota attainment, multi-year
- Experience selling to CxO level (CEO, COO, CTO, CFO)

**Nice-to-Have**:
- Selling compliance / security tools (HIPAA, SOC 2)
- Ops automation / infrastructure software experience
- Building sales teams from scratch

**Interview Process**:
- Phone screen (30 min)
- Sales scenario (60 min, mock discovery call)
- Deal review (90 min, sales director + CEO)
- Culture fit (60 min, sales team)
- Reference calls (5+ references, quota attainment probing)

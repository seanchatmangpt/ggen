KEY INSIGHTS (BRUTAL HONESTY)
================================================================================

1. INCUMBENT TRAP IS REAL
   - Every incumbent's business model fundamentally conflicts with our model
   - They profit when customers use more; we profit when they achieve outcomes
   - They cannot pivot without destroying 20-50% of revenue
   - Their inaction is rational, not stupidity

2. MARKET SHIFT IS INEVITABLE
   - 5 permanent macroeconomic forces driving change
   - Regulatory mandates (healthcare CMS, finance SEC/FINRA) creating customer demand
   - Post-SaaS fatigue making customers desperate for alternative
   - AI/ecosystem enabling outcome-indexed at scale
   - Buyer demographic shift demanding transparency

3. WE HAVE A WINDOW
   - 3-5 year window before incumbents mount serious competitive response
   - Timeline is long enough to build regulatory moat + customer lock-in
   - But not long enough to be complacent (execution matters)
   - Must achieve regulatory approval by end of 2027 (critical milestone)

4. DEFENSIBILITY IS NON-TECHNOLOGY
   - Our moat is NOT patents, architecture, or technology sophistication
   - It's regulatory approval + customer outcome history + switching cost
   - Harder to copy than features (requires 24+ months of proven outcomes)
   - Gets stronger with time (more customer data = higher switching cost)

5. EXECUTION RISK IS REAL
   - Sales cycle longer than forecast (18 months vs. 6 months possible)
   - Outcome definition complexity could scare customers
   - Regulatory approval uncertain (Big Four gatekeeping)
   - Market shift timing could slip 2-3 years
   - Incumbent aggressive response possible (70% probability)

6. OUTCOME DEFINITION IS THE BOTTLENECK
   - Not the technology (deterministic receipts, RDF ontology)
   - It's getting customers to articulate their outcomes clearly
   - It's getting auditors to accept our measurement methodology
   - Solution: Pre-built templates + LLM-assisted definition + Big Four blessing

7. FIRST CUSTOMER IS DISPROPORTIONATELY IMPORTANT
   - First healthcare system customer = proof of concept
   - Case study + reference = regulatory credibility signal
   - Enables 2-3 follow-on customers (referral effect)
   - Timeline: Must close first customer by Q4 2026 (critical)

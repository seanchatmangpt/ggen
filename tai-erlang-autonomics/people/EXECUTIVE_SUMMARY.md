### 7. Culture & Values

**6 Core Values**:
1. **First-Principles Thinking**: Solve from fundamentals, not convention
2. **Capital Efficiency**: Path to profitability > growth-at-all-costs
3. **Radical Honesty**: Speak truth, escalate problems immediately
4. **Craft & Excellence**: Build for long-term, not next demo
5. **Diversity of Thought**: Diverse perspectives, minority opinions matter
6. **Customer Obsession**: Understand deeply, but don't become driven (say no when needed)

**Operating Principles**:
- Weekly all-hands (when >5 people)
- Monthly board meetings (CEO + lead investor + independent director)
- Transparent financials (shared with all)
- No political silos (CEO involved in first 20 hires)
- Technical excellence non-negotiable (Chicago TDD, code review discipline)

---

### 8. Compensation Philosophy

**Salary Benchmarking**:
- Hire at market rates (not discount)
- Radical pay transparency (salary bands published)
- Benefits stack: Health insurance, 401k (post-Series A), PTO, home stipend
- Annual reviews with market adjustments (no negotiation)

**Equity Incentives**:
- All employees get ISOs (stock options, tax-advantaged)
- 4-year vesting / 1-year cliff (industry standard)
- Strike price = FMV at grant (avoid underwater options)
- 2% option pool Year 1 (replenish at Series A to 15-20%)

**Example**: New engineer in Year 1 gets:
- Salary: $150K
- Equity: 50,000 options (0.5% of cap table)
- Benefits: Health insurance, home office stipend, PTO
- Expected exit value: $250K-$2.5M (depending on exit multiple)

### 10. Risk Mitigation & Contingencies

**Top Risks & Mitigations**:

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| **Sales slower than expected** | 40% | High | Parallel SaaS track, extended POC timeline |
| **Series A delayed past M20** | 25% | High | Start fundraising M12 (not M18), build larger pre-seed |
| **Customer churn >15%** | 20% | High | Invest heavily in CS, monthly health checks, NPS >60 target |
| **Erlang hiring difficult** | 15% | Medium | Build contractor network, offer equity premium, focus on training |
| **Compliance barriers** | 15% | Medium | Hire Chief Compliance early (M6), seek certifications proactively |

**Spending Levers** (if runway shortens):
1. Hiring pause (save $20K/month)
2. Salary reduction (20% temp, save $200K/month)
3. Marketing freeze (save $5K/month)
4. Combined effect: 6-month runway extension

## RISK SEVERITY MATRIX

### üî¥ RED (IMMEDIATE ESCALATION)
- **Impact:** Threatens business/milestone
- **Action:** Activate contingency within 24 hours
- **Owner:** Risk owner + CEO
- **Board:** Notify same day
- **Examples:** Customer crisis, production incident, key person leaves, no deals by Week 7

### üü° AMBER (ESCALATE IF WORSENING)
- **Impact:** Could become RED if not managed
- **Action:** Monitor daily; escalate if probability increases
- **Owner:** Risk owner
- **Board:** Inform weekly
- **Examples:** Competitor in deal, burn rate tracking high, customer satisfaction declining

### üü¢ YELLOW (MAINTAIN AWARENESS)
- **Impact:** Low probability or low impact
- **Action:** Regular monitoring; maintain contingency readiness
- **Owner:** Risk owner
- **Board:** Inform monthly
- **Examples:** Funding timeline extends, economic uncertainty, churn tracking slightly high

## KEY NUMBERS TO TRACK

**Daily:**
- Pipeline: # of deals by stage (demo, POC, negotiation, signed)
- Production: Uptime %, Latency (p99), Error rate
- Team: Anyone unavailable/unhappy? (pulse check)

**Weekly:**
- Cold outreach: Response rate (target >5%)
- Sales: # of discovery calls (target 10+)
- Cash: Burn rate + runway calculation
- Customer: Satisfaction scores + NPS
- Milestones: On track? (yes/no/yellow)

**Monthly:**
- Revenue: Recurring revenue + new deals
- CAC: Customer acquisition cost
- Churn: % of customers lost
- Runway: Months remaining + burn reduction progress

**Red Lines (Activate Contingency if Hit):**
- Response rate <3%
- Burn rate >$42K/month
- Customer satisfaction <6/10
- Production downtime >10 min
- Runway <8 months
- Zero deals in pipeline by Week 6

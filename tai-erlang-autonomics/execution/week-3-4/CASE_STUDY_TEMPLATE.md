   Challenge: IT team was skeptical about changing workflow
   Solution: CFO + Chief Nursing Officer jointly endorsed the pilot
   Learning: Get leadership to co-sponsor—skepticism disappears

2. Starting with single department pilot (ICU)
   Challenge: Rolling out to all 5 departments at once was risky
   Solution: Proved concept with ICU first (high-acuity staff most resistant)
   Learning: Win over hardest department first, others follow easily

3. Daily POC check-ins (15 minutes, leadership + staff)
   Challenge: Adoption was lagging in week 2
   Solution: Daily stand-ups + celebrating small wins
   Learning: Celebrate early wins publicly—momentum matters

## Design Notes

### Formatting Tips:
- **Use charts/graphs** for before/after metrics (visual > text)
- **Highlight numbers** (dollar amounts, percentages, time savings)
- **Use customer's logo** (if permitted)
- **Include customer quote** (builds credibility)
- **Keep to 2 pages** (longer = not read)
- **Use color sparingly** (professional look)

### PDFs to Generate:
- **General version:** Generic case study (can share with prospects)
- **Customer-approved version:** Includes customer name + logo
- **Redacted version:** If customer wants to stay anonymous, remove details but keep metrics

### Distribution:
- Email to prospects (Part of follow-up sequence)
- Website case study section (Social proof)
- Sales presentations (Build confidence)
- Internal training (Sales team learning)

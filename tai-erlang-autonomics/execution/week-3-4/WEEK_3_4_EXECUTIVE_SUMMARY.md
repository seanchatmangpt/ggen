### By Segment Insights

**Enterprise SaaS Prospects (20 targeted)**
- Primary pain: Manual SKU work (90% mentioned)
- Secondary pain: Compliance audit time (80% mentioned)
- ROI Multiplier: 2-4x Year 1 (labor savings)
- Decision Timeline: 4-8 weeks typical
- Key Stakeholder: VP Product Operations / VP Revenue Operations
- **Outlook:** Strongest segment, highest conversion expected (40-50%)

**Cloud Platform / CPaaS Prospects (15 targeted)**
- Primary pain: Revenue leakage from entitlement bugs (85% mentioned)
- Secondary pain: Performance/latency at scale (75% mentioned)
- ROI Multiplier: 5-10x Year 1 (revenue protection)
- Decision Timeline: 6-8 weeks typical
- Key Stakeholder: VP Engineering / CTO / VP Billing
- **Outlook:** Highest ACV potential ($300K-750K/year), slightly longer sales cycle

**Fintech/Regulated Prospects (15 targeted)**
- Primary pain: Compliance audit requirements (95% mentioned)
- Secondary pain: Fee logic change velocity (70% mentioned)
- ROI Multiplier: 1-5x Year 1 (audit + agility)
- Decision Timeline: 8-12 weeks typical (regulatory approval)
- Key Stakeholder: VP Finance / Chief Compliance Officer / CTO
- **Outlook:** Highest regulatory barriers, strongest product-market fit signal if approved

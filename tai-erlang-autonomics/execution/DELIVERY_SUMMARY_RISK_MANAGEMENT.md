A: When a trigger is met (see QUICK_REFERENCE.md). Better early than late. If uncertain, escalate to CEO.

**Q: Who decides to activate?**

**Q: What if a contingency doesn't work?**
A: Escalate to board immediately. Move to fallback option. Document learning + update playbook.

**Q: How often to review risks?**
A: Daily (CEO), Weekly (exec team), Monthly (board), Quarterly (full assessment).

**Q: What if a new risk emerges?**
A: Add it immediately to register. Document: impact, probability, owner, mitigation, contingency.

**Q: Can we reduce contingency budget?**
A: Possibly, but only with board approval. Data-driven reduction recommended.

**Q: Share with customers?**

## State 1: Delaware (Incorporation State)

### Delaware Corporate Filing Tax

**Requirement**: Annual filing fee for Delaware C-Corporations

**Form**: Delaware Annual Report (due every year)
- **Filing Date**: Last day of June (by June 30)
- **Due Date**: June 30 each year
- **Late Fee**: 1.5% per month if late
- **Cost**: $25 annual filing fee

**Filing Method**:
1. Go to: https://dnrec.delaware.gov/divisions/corporations/
2. File: Delaware Annual Report
3. Pay: $25 online
4. Provides: Certificate of Good Standing

**Checklist**:
- [ ] Set calendar reminder for June 30 each year
- [ ] Confirm registered agent (CT Corporation) address
- [ ] Pay $25 annual filing fee
- [ ] Maintain certificate of good standing in corporate records

**Cost Summary**: $25/year

## State 2: California (If Operating in CA)

### California Franchisor Tax

**If TAI Autonomics operates in California**, must register with CA FTB (Franchise Tax Board).

#### Form: Form 568 - Nonresident Withholding Account Registration

**Registration Required IF**:
- Corporation has office/employees in California
- Corporation generates revenue from CA customers
- Corporation will file CA income tax returns

**Filing Timeline**:
- Should file within 30 days of conducting business in CA
- Before hiring first CA employee (critical!)
- Before opening CA office

**How to File**:

1. **Online via FTB**: https://onlineservices.ftb.ca.gov/
2. **Mail**: Form 568 to CA FTB, P.O. Box 1468, Sacramento, CA 95812-1468
3. **In Person**: Visit CA FTB office in Sacramento or regional office

**Form 568 Contents**:

```
FORM 568 - NONRESIDENT WITHHOLDING ACCOUNT REGISTRATION

1. BUSINESS INFORMATION:
   Business Name: TAI Autonomics, Inc.
   Federal EIN: [Your EIN]
   State of Incorporation: Delaware
   Type of Business: Software/Technology Services

2. RESPONSIBILITY INFORMATION:
   Responsible Person: [Founder Name]
   Title: CEO
   Home Address: [Address]
   Phone: [Phone]
   Email: [Email]

3. BUSINESS ADDRESS (in California):
   Address: [California office address or founder home if no office yet]
   City: [City]
   County: [County]
   ZIP: [ZIP]

4. NATURE OF BUSINESS:
   Check: "Software Development" or "Technology Services"
   Brief Description: "Autonomous AI systems for enterprise workflow"

5. BUSINESS START DATE:
   Date: [Date of incorporation]

6. EMPLOYEES:
   Number of employees: [0 initially]
   Will hire in future?: Yes / No

7. INCOME AND DEDUCTIONS:
   Estimated annual gross income: [Amount, $0 if not yet generating revenue]
   Will you have CA-source income?: Yes / No

8. SIGNATURE:
   Signature: [Your signature]
   Title: CEO
   Date: [Date]
```

**Processing Timeline**: 2-4 weeks

**Copy**: Request certified copy be mailed to you

#### Sales Tax Registration (If Applicable)

**Required IF**:
- TAI Autonomics sells physical products (unlikely for software company)
- TAI Autonomics provides taxable services in CA (depends on service type)

**For Software-Only Business**: Generally NOT required for software licensing (services may be exempt)

**To Verify Applicability**:
- Consult with CPA
- Contact CA Department of Tax and Fee Administration
- Use CA FTB Publication 1018: "A Guide to California Taxes"

#### State Income Tax Filing

**Annual Requirement**:
- File Form 100 (California Corporate Income Tax Return) if:
  - Corporation has CA-source income
  - Corporation is resident of California
  - Typically combined with federal Form 1120 filing

**Timing**: File by April 15 (or extended deadline) along with federal return

**Cost**: No filing fee; may owe taxes if profitable

## Employment Tax Registration

### California Department of Industrial Relations

**Required IF**: TAI Autonomics will hire employees

**When to Register**: BEFORE hiring first employee
- Wait until 30 days before hire date
- Register online or mail Form DE 1 (Employer Registration Form)

**Forms**:
- **Federal**: IRS Form W-4 (Employer tax withholding)
- **California**: CA Form DE 9 (Employer withholding elections)

**Timeline**: Register when ready to hire (don't register yet if pre-revenue)

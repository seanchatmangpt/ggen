## Executive Summary

An Employer Identification Number (EIN) is required for:
- Opening a business bank account
- Filing taxes (even with no employees)
- Hiring employees or contractors
- Forming LLCs or partnerships
- Establishing retirement plans

TAI Autonomics will apply for an EIN immediately after receiving the Certificate of Incorporation.

---

## Application Methods

### Method 1: Online (RECOMMENDED - Same Day)

**Fastest**: Apply online at IRS.gov, receive EIN immediately

#### Steps:

1. **Go to**: https://www.irs.gov/ein
2. **Verify Eligibility**:
   - US business entity
   - No prior EIN
   - Valid Social Security Number (sole proprietor/owner)
   - Mailing address in US

3. **Complete Form SS-4 Online**:
   - Business name: TAI Autonomics, Inc.
   - Business address: [Founder address initially, update after first office]
   - Entity type: C Corporation
   - Reason for applying: New business
   - Type of entity: Corporation
   - Does applicant have SSN? Yes
   - Applicant SSN: [Founder SSN]
   - Has business operated? No
   - Accounting period: Calendar year (Jan 1 - Dec 31)
   - First date wages paid: N/A (will update if hiring)

4. **Submit & Receive**:
   - Takes ~2 minutes
   - EIN issued immediately on screen
   - Confirmation email sent within 24 hours
   - **Screenshot this page** - you'll need EIN for bank account

**Estimated Time**: 15 minutes
**Cost**: Free

## After EIN Received

### Immediate Actions:

1. **Save EIN Documentation**
   - Take screenshot of IRS confirmation page
   - Save confirmation email
   - Store in: `/execution/week-1-2/legal-docs/02_EIN_Confirmation.pdf`

2. **Update Corporate Records**
   - Add EIN to cap table template
   - Add EIN to bank account application forms
   - Add EIN to any vendor/contractor agreements

3. **Notify Bank**
   - Bank account application requires EIN
   - You're now ready for parallel bank account processing

4. **IRS Verification**
   - IRS ITIN system takes 24-48 hours to sync
   - Wait 1-2 business days before bank account attempts (recommended)
   - If bank rejects, retry after 48 hours

### EIN Best Practices:

- [ ] **Guard EIN like SSN** - Don't share with non-essential third parties
- [ ] **Update IRS** - If business address changes, file Form 8822-B within 60 days
- [ ] **Keep record** - Never lose EIN; it's permanent for TAI Autonomics, Inc.
- [ ] **Bank memo** - Paste EIN in computer memo app and phone for quick reference

## RESOLUTION 13: CONFLICT OF INTEREST POLICY

**WHEREAS**, the Board of Directors deems it important to maintain ethical standards and prevent conflicts of interest;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, that the Board of Directors hereby adopts the following Conflict of Interest Policy:

1. **Disclosure Required**: Any director, officer, or employee with a material interest in any transaction with the Corporation shall immediately disclose such interest to the Board

2. **Approval Process**: Interested parties shall:
   - Abstain from voting on related matters
   - Recuse from discussions involving the transaction
   - Provide full transparency regarding the transaction terms

3. **Permitted Transactions**: A transaction involving a director or officer shall not be voided if:
   - Full disclosure was made
   - Remaining board members approved in good faith
   - The transaction was fair to the Corporation at fair market value

4. **Violations**: Violations of this policy may result in:
   - Forfeiture of shares or compensation
   - Removal from office
   - Potential legal action

TAI Autonomics, Inc. will be incorporated as a Delaware C-Corporation. This provides:
- Favorable corporate law (separate P&L for each subsidiary, flexible stock structures)

## Phase 2: Filing Documents (Day 2)

### Document 1: Certificate of Incorporation

```
STATE OF DELAWARE
CERTIFICATE OF INCORPORATION

1. NAME: TAI Autonomics, Inc.

2. ADDRESS OF REGISTERED OFFICE: 251 Little Falls Drive, Wilmington, DE 19808

3. REGISTERED AGENT: CT Corporation, Delaware

4. INCORPORATOR:
   Name: [Founder Name]
   Address: [Founder Address]

5. AUTHORIZED SHARES:
   - Common Stock: 10,000,000 shares, par value $0.0001/share
   - Preferred Stock: 5,000,000 shares, par value $0.0001/share

6. BOARD OF DIRECTORS:
   The corporation shall have one (1) director initially, to be designated
   by the incorporator. The number of directors may be increased or
   decreased by action of the Board of Directors.

7. BYLAWS: The Board of Directors shall have authority to adopt, amend,
   or repeal the Bylaws of the corporation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the undersigned Incorporator has executed this
Certificate of Incorporation as of [DATE].

_____________________________
[Incorporator Signature]
[Incorporator Name]
[Date]
```

### Filing Method: Online via Delaware DGCL

1. Go to: https://dnrec.delaware.gov/divisions/corporations/
2. Select: "Online Filing System"
3. Create account (if new)
4. File: "Certificate of Incorporation - For Profit"
5. Upload signed PDF or use e-signature via DocuSign
6. Pay fee: $107.50 (online payment accepted)
7. Receive: File number and certified copies within 24 hours

**Alternative**: Use registered agent (CT Corp) - they will file for you ($299 all-in)

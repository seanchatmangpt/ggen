INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

ERRORS & OMISSIONS (E&O):
  - Minimum Limits: $1M per claim / $2M aggregate
  - Critical: Contractual liability endorsement (required for MSAs)
  - Coverage: Software development, consulting, integration
  - Excludes: Pure product warranty (we do services)
  - Budget: $8K-$15K/year

DIRECTORS & OFFICERS (D&O):
  - Minimum Limits: $500K per claim / $1M aggregate
  - Important: Side A coverage (insured vs insured)
  - Coverage: Board liability, management decisions
  - Investor requirement: Almost 100% of VCs require
  - Budget: $3K-$5K/year

CYBER LIABILITY:
  - Minimum Limits: $500K coverage
  - Coverage: Data breach, ransomware, business interruption
  - Critical: Customer data protection prerequisite
  - Budget: $2K-$4K/year

COMMERCIAL GENERAL LIABILITY:
  - Minimum Limits: $1M per occurrence / $2M aggregate
  - Coverage: Bodily injury, property damage, personal injury
  - Required: For physical office operations
  - Budget: $2K-$3K/year

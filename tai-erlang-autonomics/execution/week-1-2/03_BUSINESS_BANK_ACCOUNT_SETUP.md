## Executive Summary

TAI Autonomics requires a dedicated business bank account for:
- Separating personal and business finances (legal requirement)
- Recording business income/expenses (IRS compliance)
- Hiring contractors and paying vendors
- Establishing business credit history
- Managing capital contributions and distributions

**Recommendation**: Silicon Valley Bank (SVB) or Founders Bank - both specialize in early-stage tech startups with minimal balances.

---

## SVB Account Opening Checklist

### Pre-Opening (Day 1)

- [ ] **Have Documents Ready**:
  - Original/certified Certificate of Incorporation (physical copy)
  - Federal EIN confirmation (screenshot from IRS)
  - Founder government ID (driver's license or passport)
  - Founder address proof (utility bill or lease agreement <90 days old)
  - Founder phone number and email

- [ ] **Gather Financial Information**:
  - Initial capital contribution amount (minimum $500, recommend $5,000)
  - Business purpose (code generation, AI services)
  - Expected monthly transactions
  - Will you be processing credit cards? (No initially)

- [ ] **Prepare Address**:
  - If no office yet: Use founder's home address initially
  - Plan to update after securing office space
  - Note: Bank can mail checks to different address if needed

### Online Application (Day 1-2)

#### Step-by-Step:

1. **Go to SVB Startup Banking Portal**
   - URL: https://www.svb.com/startup-banking
   - Click: "Open an Account"

2. **Select Account Type**
   - Choose: "Checking Account - Startup"
   - Industry: "Software/Technology"
   - Entity Type: "C Corporation"

3. **Enter Business Information**
   - Business name: TAI Autonomics, Inc.
   - Entity type: C Corporation
   - Incorporation date: [Date from Certificate]
   - Incorporation state: Delaware
   - EIN: [Your EIN from Form SS-4]
   - Business address: [Current address or founder's home]
   - Website: [If available: www.tai-autonomics.com]
   - Business description: "Autonomous AI systems for enterprise workflow"
   - Annual revenue projection: $0 (new business)

4. **Enter Responsible Party Information**
   - Name: [Founder Legal Name]
   - Title: CEO/Founder
   - Ownership %: 100%
   - Date of birth: [Founder DOB]
   - SSN: [Founder SSN]
   - Home address: [Founder home address]
   - Phone: [Founder phone]
   - Email: [Founder email]

5. **Upload Documents**
   - Certificate of Incorporation (PDF scan)
   - EIN Confirmation letter (PDF screenshot)
   - Driver's license/passport (front & back)
   - Address proof (utility bill or lease)
   - Form W-9 (will be provided by bank)

6. **Verify Information**
   - Review all entered data
   - Agree to terms of service
   - E-sign opening documents (DocuSign)

7. **Submit & Wait**
   - Application submitted
   - SVB will review within 24-48 hours
   - If approved, account activated immediately

### After Approval (Day 3-5)

#### Online Banking Setup:

1. **Activate Online Access**
   - SVB emails login credentials
   - Set up two-factor authentication (SMS + security question)
   - Download SVB mobile app (optional but recommended)

2. **Fund Account**
   - Initial deposit: Transfer minimum $500 (recommend $5,000)
   - Via ACH transfer: SVB provides routing number and account number
   - Via wire transfer: SVB provides wire instructions
   - Via check deposit: Mobile deposit available via app

3. **Order Checks**
   - SVB provides free first order of checks
   - Standard checks ordered and arrive in 5-7 business days
   - Alternatively: Use digital payments (ACH, wire, debit card)

4. **Set Up Online Payments**
   - Link other bank accounts for transfers
   - Enable ACH payments to vendors
   - Set up bill pay (if needed)

5. **Request Debit Card**
   - SVB provides business debit card
   - Use for business expenses only
   - Reconcile monthly statements

#### Compliance & Setup:

1. **W-9 on File**
   - SVB requires W-9 for all accounts
   - You'll complete during application or email before funding

2. **Business Registration with State** (CA, if applicable)
   - File Form 568 with CA FTB if doing business in CA
   - EIN required (you have it)
   - Timeline: 1-2 weeks processing

3. **Update Corporate Records**
   - Save bank account number and routing number
   - Store in `/execution/week-1-2/legal-docs/03_SVB_Bank_Account_Setup.md`
   - Add to cap table template

## Critical: After Bank Account Opened

### Update All Documents:

1. **Corporate Records**
   - Add bank account number to cap table
   - Document account opening in corporate minute book

2. **IRS/State**
   - Bank account info not reported to IRS (FYI only)
   - CA FTB Form 568 if doing business in CA (EIN required)

3. **Vendors/Contractors**
   - Update business address if changed
   - Provide SVB banking details for payment ACH transfers

4. **Compliance**
   - Maintain separate business banking
   - Do NOT mix personal and business funds
   - Reconcile monthly statements

## One-Page Overview

**TAI** = autonomous inventory + supply chain management platform that automatically optimizes SKU movement, pricing, and margin realization using value-indexed governance.

**Why Now?**
- $50B+ TAM in e-commerce inventory management alone
- Manual inventory coordination costs: 3-5% of GMV
- Margin leakage: $10-50M+ for mid-market retailers
- No production-grade autonomic solution exists today

**Our Technology:**
- Erlang/OTP battle-tested runtime (telecom, financial trading)
- Real-time event streaming (Pub/Sub)
- Cryptographic audit ledger (compliance-ready)
- Self-healing governors (autonomous decision-making)

**Unit Economics (Exceptional):**
- **Gross Margin:** 98.8%
- **CAC Payback:** 10 months
- **LTV/CAC Ratio:** 88x (best-in-class SaaS = 3-5x)

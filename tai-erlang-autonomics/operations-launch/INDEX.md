## üí° Key Principles

1. **Preparation Over Heroics**
   - Completed checklist 5 days before (not 5 minutes)
   - Runbooks practiced before launch day
   - Team confident and well-rested

2. **Transparency Over Silence**
   - Status page updated every 30 minutes
   - Customers told the truth about what happened
   - No surprises

3. **Automation Over Manual**
   - Backups automated (not manual)
   - Alerts automated (not manual checking)
   - Payment retries automated (not support team work)

4. **Prevention Over Firefighting**
   - Incident runbooks prevent mistakes
   - Monitoring catches issues early
   - Fallback strategies prevent cascades

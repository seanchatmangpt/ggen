## Key Metrics Definitions

**Pipeline**: Total $ value of prospects in CRM (all stages)
**Qualified Leads**: ICP Score 75+, ready for discovery
**POC Candidate**: After discovery, scored 8-10, agree to 30-day POC
**Sales Cycle**: Days from first call to contract signature (target: 45 avg)
**Win Rate**: % of POC candidates that close (target: 25-30%)
**CAC**: Customer acquisition cost = Sales + marketing spend / # customers

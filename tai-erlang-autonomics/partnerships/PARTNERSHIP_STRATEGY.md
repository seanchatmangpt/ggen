## Risk Management & Mitigation

### Key Risks
1. **Partner Cannibalization**: Direct sales vs channel conflicts
   - *Mitigation*: Clear territory allocation, conflict resolution committee

2. **Technology Incompatibility**: Integration challenges with partner platforms
   - *Mitigation*: Early technical alignment, dedicated integration teams

3. **Competitive Positioning**: Partners with competing code generation tools
   - *Mitigation*: Exclusive arrangements for premium offerings, tech advantage

4. **Dependency Risk**: Over-reliance on single partner
   - *Mitigation*: Diversified partner portfolio, no single partner >20% revenue

5. **Regulatory Changes**: Compliance requirements vary by jurisdiction
   - *Mitigation*: Proactive standards tracking, flexible partnership terms

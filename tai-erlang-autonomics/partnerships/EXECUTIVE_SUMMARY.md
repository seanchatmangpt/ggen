## Comparison: Direct Sales vs Partnership Model

### Direct Sales Model
- **Sales Cycle**: 6-12 months per enterprise deal
- **CAC**: $5,000-10,000 per customer
- **LTV**: $50,000+ per customer (3-5 year contracts)
- **CAC Payback**: 6-12 months
- **Scalability**: Bottleneck at sales team size (100+ salespeople = $500K+ cost)
- **Annual Revenue from 10 salespeople**: $5-10M

### Partnership Model
- **Sales Cycle**: 2-4 months (partners own relationships)
- **CAC**: $2,000 per customer
- **LTV**: $35,000+ per customer (more predictable)
- **CAC Payback**: 2-3 months
- **Scalability**: 50+ partners × 20+ customers/partner/year = 1000+ customers
- **Annual Revenue from 50 partnerships**: $140M+

**Winner**: Partnership model achieves 10x+ scale at 1/5th the cost.

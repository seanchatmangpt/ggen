## Risk Mitigation Checklist

### Partnership Execution Risks
- [ ] **Contingency partnerships** identified (backup if primary partner stalls)
- [ ] **Conflict resolution process** documented
- [ ] **Territory allocation** clearly defined (no overlapping accounts)
- [ ] **Escalation paths** established (for issues/disputes)
- [ ] **Partner SLAs** documented (response times, support levels)
- [ ] **Performance metrics** clearly defined (what success looks like)

### Technical Integration Risks
- [ ] **API stability guarantees** from cloud platforms
- [ ] **Early warning system** for platform changes
- [ ] **Contingency integration approaches** documented
- [ ] **Performance testing** of integrations planned
- [ ] **Security review** of integrations completed
- [ ] **Data privacy compliance** verified (GDPR, CCPA)

### Business Risks
- [ ] **Revenue concentration** limits (no partner >20% ARR)
- [ ] **Diversified partner portfolio** (multiple partners per tier)
- [ ] **Contract terms** reviewed by legal (confidentiality, IP, liability)
- [ ] **Force majeure** clauses included (pandemic, natural disaster)
- [ ] **Termination procedures** clearly defined (wind-down, transition)
- [ ] **Dispute resolution process** established (arbitration, escalation)

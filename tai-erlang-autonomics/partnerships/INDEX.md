### 5. PARTNER_AGREEMENTS.md (33 KB, 1,143 lines)
**Master Agreement Templates**

**Quick Start**: Ready-to-customize agreement templates for all partner tiers.

**Templates Included**:

1. **Cloud Platform Partnership** (AWS/Azure/GCP)
   - Duration: 3 years with annual renewal
   - Revenue share: 25-30% ggen
   - Sections: Overview, Commercial Terms, Technical Integration, GTM, Governance, IP, Dispute Resolution
   - Estimated customization time: 4-6 weeks with legal

2. **Systems Integrator Partnership** (Accenture/Deloitte/IBM)
   - Duration: 3 years with annual renewal
   - Revenue share: 10-15% ggen (services)
   - Sections: Commercial terms, Vertical solutions, Delivery standards, Certification, Quality metrics
   - Estimated customization time: 3-4 weeks

3. **Compliance & Audit Partnership** (Big Four)
   - Duration: 3 years with annual renewal
   - Revenue share: 15-20% ggen (compliance services)
   - Sections: Compliance solutions, Auditor enablement, Receipt framework, Governance
   - Estimated customization time: 4-6 weeks

4. **Consultancy Partnership** (McKinsey/Bain/BCG)
   - Revenue share: 5-10% of influenced transformation deals
   - Sections: Strategic value, co-marketing, executive alignment

5. **Academic Research Partnership** (Stanford/MIT/CMU)
   - Research grants: $250K-1M annually
   - Sections: Research scope, IP sharing, publication rights, student engagement, technology licensing
   - Estimated customization time: 2-3 weeks

6. **Technology Integration Partnership** (Databricks/Confluent/LLM providers)
   - Revenue share: 3-5% of integrated solutions
   - Sections: API integration, technical documentation, revenue sharing

7. **Reseller & Channel Partnership**
   - Territory-based resellers
   - Margin: 30-40% software, 10-20% services
   - Sections: Territory allocation, pricing, support, performance metrics

8. **Standard Terms** (all agreements)
   - Data & Privacy (GDPR, CCPA compliance)
   - Insurance & Liability (minimum coverage requirements)
   - Compliance & Legal (sanctions, export controls, anti-corruption)
   - Force Majeure
   - Confidentiality (3-year period)
   - Dispute Resolution (escalation → arbitration)
   - Term & Termination (180-day wind-down)

**Key Features**:
- Modular structure (mix and match sections)
- Bracketed placeholders for customization
- Standard financial terms by tier
- Governance templates
- KPI definitions and measurement
- MDF (Marketing Development Fund) allocation formulas
- Clear escalation and dispute resolution processes

**Who Should Read**: Legal team, VP of Partnerships, deal negotiators

**Time to Read**: 60-90 minutes (full), 15-20 minutes (skim)

**Usage**: Customize templates with specific partner names, dates, and financial terms

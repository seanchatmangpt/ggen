KEY DECISIONS EXPLAINED
=======================

Why Email for MVP (Not Intercom)?
  - Cheap ($0 vs $50/mo)
  - Async (no real-time staffing)
  - Searchable (customer can find old answers)
  - Integrates with Slack (best of both worlds)

Why 24h Response for Starter Tier?
  - $2.5K/month customer = $200/month budget for support
  - Business hours response acceptable
  - Reduces need for evening coverage
  - Still professional, meets expectations

Why 1-hour for Enterprise?
  - $75K+/month tier has $6K+ per ticket budget
  - LTV = $1.8M (60 months × $30K net margin)
  - Prevents $180K+ churn if issue takes down production
  - ROI is 36,000x ($5 phone call prevents $180K loss)

Why Auto-Resolve 45% of Tickets?
  - Common issues are predictable
  - API errors = 15% of tickets
  - Quota/rate limit = 10% of tickets
  - Malformed JSON = 8% of tickets
  - Result: 12 hours/week = $75K/year saved

Why Service Credits (Not Cash Refunds)?
  - Refunds erode margins (lost revenue)
  - Credits incentivize stay (use next month)
  - Automatic (better optics than manual claims)
  - Limited (10-50% max prevents abuse)

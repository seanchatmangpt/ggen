## Why This Matters

### Traditional Academic Paper Development

Author writes paper → submits → gets reviews → revises manually → resubmits

**Problem**: Equations are static strings. Change one equation, break cross-references, tables, discussions. High error rate.

### Semantic Paper Development (ggen approach)

Define paper in RDF → add equations as triples → LaTeX auto-generates

**Advantage**: Change equation in RDF → everything updates automatically. Zero manual editing. Perfect consistency.

================================================================================
                           ALERT SPECIFICATIONS
================================================================================

ALERT #1: SupervisorHighRestartRate (CRITICAL)
  Expression: rate(supervisor_restart_count[1m]) > 5
  Duration: 1 minute before firing
  Action: Page on-call engineer immediately
  Runbook: Supervisor Restart Spike
  Impact: High application instability, may lead to cascading failures
  Recovery Time: 5-30 minutes

ALERT #2: CircuitBreakerOpen (WARNING)
  Expression: circuit_breaker_state == 1
  Duration: 30 seconds before firing
  Action: Warn platform team
  Runbook: Circuit Breaker Open
  Impact: Downstream service unavailable
  Recovery Time: 10-60 minutes

ALERT #3: ConsensusQuorumLost (CRITICAL)
  Expression: (healthy_nodes / total_nodes) < 0.6666
  Duration: 2 minutes before firing
  Action: Declare incident, page entire on-call team
  Runbook: Consensus Quorum Loss
  Impact: CRITICAL - Cannot make distributed decisions, system halted
  Recovery Time: 15-120 minutes (incident-level severity)

ALERT #4: EventStoreHighLatency (WARNING)
  Expression: histogram_quantile(0.99, ...) > 1.0s
  Duration: 5 minutes before firing
  Action: Warn platform team
  Runbook: Event Store Latency
  Impact: Slow event persistence, potential user-facing latency
  Recovery Time: 10-45 minutes
